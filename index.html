Welcome to Mapfit! 🗺️

Mapfit is your go-to app for tracking workouts on the map. Here's a quick guide:

  1. Add a New Workout: Click on the map and fill in type, distance, and duration. 🏃‍♂️

  5. Delete All Workouts: Clear all workouts at once. 🗑️
